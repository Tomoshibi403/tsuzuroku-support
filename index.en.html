Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What happens to my Premium purchase if I switch devices or reinstall the app?

A. No additional payment is required. You can restore it with the same Apple ID from "Restore Purchases" in Settings → "Premium".

Q. Where are my recorded times and notes stored?

A. Everything is stored on your device. Nothing is sent to any external server (see the Privacy Policy for details).

Q. What if I made a mistake or forgot to record my time?

A. You can manually add or edit time from the Stopwatch screen. You can also use this to migrate time you tracked in the past.

Q. I'm not sure how to use the app.

A. In the app, open the top-left menu → "info" → "How to Use", where you'll find a "Quick Guide" for first-time users and a "Detailed Guide" that explains each screen in depth.
