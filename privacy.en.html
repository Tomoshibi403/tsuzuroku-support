Privacy Policy

Tsuzuroku app / Last updated: June 14, 2026

Tomoshibi, the developer of the time-tracking app "Tsuzuroku" (hereinafter "the Developer"), respects and is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This policy explains how the app ("the App") handles user information.

Basic Policy

The App does not collect or transmit users' personal information. Data such as tasks, recorded time, notes, and various settings are stored only on the user's device. The Developer cannot view or obtain any of this data.

Data Stored on Your Device

Data created in the App (task names, recorded time, notes, goal settings, app display preferences, and so on) is stored in local storage on the user's device. It is not transmitted to any external server.

About Photos

The feature that lets you set a photo as a task's background stores only the image you select on your device. The App does not access your entire photo library, nor does it send images externally.

About Notifications

Work and break timer notifications and reminders are all local notifications generated on your device. No personal information is sent externally for notifications.

About Purchases (Premium)

The App's one-time Premium purchase is processed through Apple's App Store and StoreKit. Payment information is managed by Apple, and the Developer does not receive your payment details. Checking and restoring your purchase status is also handled through Apple's mechanisms.

About iCloud and Backups

Your device's iCloud backup, and any sync data if the App supports iCloud sync, are stored within your own iCloud (Apple) account. These are under Apple's management, and the Developer cannot access them.

Third Parties, Analytics, and Advertising

The App does not use any third-party analytics tools or advertising frameworks. It does not provide or sell your information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App is not designed to collect personal information from any specific age group. As stated above, it does not collect personal information in the first place.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be revised as needed. If there are significant changes, we will announce them on this page.
